Welcome to Ritual!

I've always been fairly goal-oriented. Despite this, it took me a while to realize that I was routinely forgetting the distance I'd covered each week; the leaps and bounds of my efforts, lost in my throes of emotional uncertainty. Journaling seemed to be the solution, but it was too time consuming for what came of it.

This is where Ritual comes in--remembering and learning from your week through weekly newsletters on what you've accomplished and how you can better move toward your goals.

How to Use Ritual

Ritual is intended as a journaling software where you put down your thoughts and forget about them until the newsletter rolls around. Interacting with Ritual takes place entirely through email--every time you have a thought, activity, feeling, or anything else you want a response on, send an email to ritual@joeytan.dev. The contents of that email will then be considered in the newsletter's generation.

As short-term goals are important for incremental improvements, Ritual will suggest simple goals for you to tackle in the coming week based on what you've logged already.

Please enjoy using Ritual. If you have any questions, let me know at j.tan2231@gmail.com.
