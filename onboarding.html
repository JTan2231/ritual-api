Welcome to Ritual!

I've always been fairly goal-oriented. Despite this, it took me a while to realize that I was routinely forgetting the distance I'd covered each week; the leaps and bounds of my efforts, lost in my throes of emotional uncertainty. Journaling seemed to be the solution, but it was too time consuming for what came of it.

This is where Ritual comes in--remembering and learning from your week through weekly newsletters on what you've accomplished and how you can better move toward your goals.

How to Use Ritual

In Ritual, you are in charge of two components: Activities and Goals.

Your newsletter will include commentary on the relevance your activities to your Goals; in other words, how well are you working toward the goals you set out to achieve? To set your goals in Ritual, reply to this email message with some goals you wish to focus on. For example--and keep in mind, formatting isn't important; it's about the content:

- fitness--gotta stay active and healthy to maintain high energy for other endeavours
- programming--a healthy, creative, and robust set of projects that showcase my product and technical abilities
- tekken--comfortably in purple ranks with a few characters
- piano--chopin scherzos and less intense parlor pieces
- ritual--robust set of features; clean/usable UI

Activities are the events that you log throughout your week that you want Ritual to remember. For example, suppose you spent the day playing Tekken and piano and you wanted to relay that to Ritual--you would then send an email to ritual@joeytan.dev with something like the following:

Played Tekken--can't sidestep to save my life. Need to be more wary of lows, maybe start using low parries? Not sure how to practice outside fights.

Practiced the scherzo and liebestraum--really seeing the fruits of slow practice, need to continue; need better practice routines on the jumps, why do they feel so inconsistent?

You can send emails like these as often as you like. The details on each activity logged will then be considered in the generation of your weekly newsletter.
